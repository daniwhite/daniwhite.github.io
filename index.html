About me

Robotics
I spent my lat 3 years of high school on the South Eugene Robotics Team. My primary role was writing code for the team's robot in Java. During the last two I served as software lead, coordinating the efforts of our team of programmers. Our code incorporated a variety of topics, including PID control and machine vision.
School
In the fall I'll be a freshman at MIT. I'm planning on majoring in Course 6 (computer science/electrical engineering). During my last two years of high school I also took classes at the University or Oregon, including discrete math, combinatorics, multivariable calculus, and differential equations.
